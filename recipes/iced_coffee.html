Iced Coffee


Description

This iced coffee is perfect for hot summer days when getting together with friends.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 cup coffee, at room temperature or chilled
  • 1 tbsp cream or milk
  • 1 to 2 tsp white sugar

Steps:

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Pour in coffee
  3. Add cream and sugar
  4. Stir and drink!
